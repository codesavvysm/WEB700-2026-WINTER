overflow Property

When content is larger than its container, overflow controls what happens: clipping and/or scrollbars.

overflow: visible (default)

visible

Content can spill outside the box. No scrollbars, nothing clipped.

overflow: hidden

hidden

Content is clipped. No scrollbars. Extra content is not visible.

overflow: scroll

scroll

Scrollbars are always shown (horizontal and vertical), content is clipped and scrollable.

overflow: auto

auto

Scrollbars appear only when needed. Content is clipped and scrollable when necessary.
